Health calculators that show their work

We built ProteinCalc because most "protein calculators" online give you one flat number with no explanation of where it came from.

Our approach

Every calculator on this site is built on a named, referenced formula — Mifflin-St Jeor for metabolic rate, the Boer formula for lean body mass, the Devine formula for ideal weight — rather than a proprietary black box. We link the reasoning, not just the result.

Who this is for

Anyone who wants a personalized starting point for their nutrition: people training for strength or physique goals, people managing weight, and people who just want to understand their own numbers better.

What we're not

We're not a substitute for a doctor, registered dietitian, or physical therapist. Our tools give general, population-level estimates — always check with a qualified professional for anything specific to your health history.
